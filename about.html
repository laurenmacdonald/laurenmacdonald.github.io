I'm Lauren.

I used to work in research, now I work with code.

Over the past two years, I've dug into backend and frontend programming, software, API, web and mobile development. I've gained knowledge in data structures, database management and architecture, programming languages (such as Java, SQL, HTML, CSS, Kotlin, C, C++, C#, to name a few), Object-Oriented Programming and SOLID design principles. During a summer co-op at CGI, I applied my knowledge of database architecture and management to a government client contract where I assisted a data engineering government team with their ETL development process.

My technical expertise is complemented by a Bachelor of Arts in Honors Psychology from Saint Mary's University, where I focused on psychology research. I translated that expertise to 7 years in healthcare and implementation research, working in topics ranging from Alzheimer's Disease clinical trials to in-community COVID-19 response initiatives before returning to post-secondary education.

The problem-solving nature of programming and its potential to help people drew me in, aligning with my passion for learning and purposeful work. I hope to extend my interdisciplinary background to meaningful projects, approaching each with curiosity and engagement.
